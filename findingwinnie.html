Finding Winnie: The Story of the World's Most Famous Bear by Lindsay Mattick

Two Winnies,

    two stories,

    a smooth transition from one to the other.

Two fairy tale openings—

    one TRUE true story

    one real bear, and one stuffed.

Two degrees of separation,

    two stories within two stories—

    a wonderful framing device.

A whiff of Nevil Shute

    but here a fluid frame.

    a movement in and out.

A story celebrating Winnie and Pooh Bear.

Title: Finding Winnie: The Story of the World's Most Famous Bear

Author: Lindsay Mattick

Illustrator: Sophie Blackall

Publisher: Little, Brown and Company

Hardback: 52 pages

ISBN: 978-0-376-32490-8
